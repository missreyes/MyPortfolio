Pet sitting rates and services
We offer in home pet sitting, as well as pet sitting at the home of one of our pet care professionals. We do not provide kennel service, and sitting is available on a first reserve first serve basis. Priority is always given to full subscription customers. Those not on subscriptions will only be considered for pet sitting, after initial assessment of pet’s needs and temperament. Rates for pet sitting vary depending on location and required walks, feeding schedule, medications, etc. A minimum of $20 for a half hour cat visit includes feeding, playing, petting, brushing, plant watering, mail pickup, litter pan cleaning, etc.; up to $90 per day for dogs requiring daily walks and an overnight stay.

At this time we don’t offer a half-hour walk. We feel most dogs need more than a half-hour of exercise daily. Even small dogs, enjoy time outdoors, and our goal at NYC Pet Nanny is to provide the most effective walking service for your dog’s good health. But, why hire a walking service for a short half-hour walk, when for nearly the same price you could have a trained NYC Pet Nanny walk your dog for a full hour. Our walks are a minimum of one hour long, and often the walks are up to two hours in length (depending on the route, time spent in dog runs, and your dog’s physical condition, as well as your input as the dog’s owner).
